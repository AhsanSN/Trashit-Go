    About trash it

    Trash it is a social and tech startup founded by young, energetic youth of Pakistan. We enable residents of Pakistan to efficiently dispose their trash by adopting composting and other ways. We aim to empower people to transition towards a zero waste lifestyle. Our main focus is on reusing kitchen waste (also known as green waste) and paper scraps (referring to brown waste) to make organic compost - khaad. If each household starts composting, imagine the difference we will be able to achieve as a community.
